The Mechanism15

Governance decision

The IM/DM decides which pattern.
The agent can't.

01AI Drafts → Human ReviewsUse: code, docs, plans · IM role: define review gates in DoD
02Human Steers → AI ExecutesUse: complex analysis, research · IM role: maintain backlog agents pull from
03AI Monitors → Human IntervenesUse: ops, alerts, QA · IM role: set escalation thresholds

Pattern selection is workflow design — not an AI-augmented decision. Malone's finding applies to decisions made with AI. The IM/DM selects the pattern before the work begins.

Governance Maturity20

Where most organisations sit today

Most teams are at L1 or L2. Moving to L3 is governance work — and that is the IM/DM's territory.

L1Unseen AINo inventory of agents in use
L2Observed AIVisibility — no control
L3Controlled AIPolicies enforced, owners assigned
L4Autonomous GovernanceAgents monitoring agents

L2 → L3: assign ownership, design HITL patterns, set escalation thresholds. Delivery governance, not technical work.
