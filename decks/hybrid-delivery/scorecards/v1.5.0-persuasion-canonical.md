## What moved the score

**The CTA — the deck's persistent ceiling across every prior version — is finally fixed.** The closing frame (Slide 26) now carries a concrete first action ("Start with the squad audit — this week") *and* a strong ownership-activating forward close: "The capability gap closes on its own. The governance gap is yours to close." That line does real work — it ties the deck's two ladders together and hands the audience the lever they hold. CTA 6.0 → 7.5. This is the headline persuasion gain and the main driver of the +0.3 overall.

**The reorder front-loads urgency and self-location.** Moving the squad gap (441%) to Slide 6 and the softened "locate yourself" to Slide 7 means the audience sees itself in the problem early — the "see themselves before they're asked to act" structure Aria praised in v1.1.0, now at the front. The new **Agent Capability Maturity ladder (Slide 3)** gives capability builders a vocabulary ladder and the human-role-escalation through-line from the start.

**More usable vocabulary, fewer skeptic frictions.** The VERDICT→cadence mapping (Slide 21) gives the IM concrete, ceremony-anchored language to use on Monday. Honest on-slide VERDICT attribution and the explicit population on Slide 14 ("of agile teams") remove two skeptic friction points. IM-only framing tightens the audience focus.

---

## What's traded or at risk

**Emotional resonance held flat (7.5), not raised — a deliberate register shift.** v1.1.0 explicitly praised the Wave 1 slides as "lived-experience recognition." Removing the Wave narrative removes that recognition beat — the "you lived this in 2022" moment. That is a real loss of one emotional hook. It is offset by (a) the forward-looking, less-doom framing the audience-owner asked for, and (b) the early squad-gap urgency, which lands the "engineers are ahead" recognition hit sooner. Net: the deck trades a backward-looking recognition beat for forward-looking ownership. Even, not down.

**Front-loading the problem before the solution.** Squad gap + locate-yourself now appear before the boundary and mechanisms are taught. Softening locate-yourself to plain language mitigates the risk of early, unresolved anxiety; narrative clarity held at 8.5.

**VERDICT may not fully convince the structural-skeptic ~15%.** Single-source practitioner framework; honest on-slide attribution limits the damage, but it is not the multi-source incident evidence those skeptics respond to best.
