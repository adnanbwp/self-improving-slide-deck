OECD TALIS, 2024
Fewer than 1 in 3 schools has a formal AI policy.
Across 38 participating countries, the OECD Teaching and Learning International Survey found that the overwhelming majority of classroom teachers have received no structured preparation for navigating AI tools. The gap is widest in secondary education, where AI use by students is already routine.
Source: OECD TALIS 2024 — Teaching and Learning International Survey 01
