aha agile — design system
Design System

aha agile

A duotone editorial / Swiss-brutalist poster system. Two fields — burnt orange and warm cream — divided by a hard horizontal seam. Oversized numerals bleed off the edge, monospace metadata sits in the margins, and a single heavy grotesque headline anchors the composition. Sharp, dry, deadpan.

01 — Colour

Duotone + Ink

Two dominant fields carry the whole system; ink does all the typographic work. Tints are field-specific — never mix an orange tint onto the cream field.

02 — Typography

Grotesque × Mono

Two families only. Archivo at black weight (900) for display and numerals — tight tracking, near-solid leading. Floor: multi-line mixed-case display text needs leading ≥ 0.96 (Archivo's descender + cap height ≈ 0.92em — anything tighter collides); only single lines and numerals may run tighter. Space Mono for every label, caption and value — small, with the typewriter texture that gives the system its archival voice.
