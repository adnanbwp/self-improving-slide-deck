# Avatar Portrait Brief — myPKA Team v2

- **Date:** 2026-05-29
- **Model:** fal-ai/nano-banana-2 (all entries)
- **Aspect ratio:** 1:1
- **Source:** avatars/avatar_research/character_definition.json

## Style thread

Pencil sketch portrait illustrations on white — graphite hatching and crosshatching throughout, realistic-leaning faces with full facial rendering, hair in grouped sketch masses, and each character's single accent colour restricted entirely to the icon circle borders and dot markers of their 8-icon semicircular orbit arc. All twelve portraits form a coherent visual family through shared sketch medium, whitespace discipline, and 3/4-angle bust framing, differentiated only by unique silhouette, posture energy, expression, and symbol language.

### Adnan (adnan)

**Accent:** Warm Ochre (#C49A30)

**Prompt:**

CORE STYLE

Pencil sketch portrait illustration, graphite on white.
Realistic rendering — fine pencil hatching and crosshatching.
Skin with light sketch texture.
Hair rendered as grouped sketch masses with directional line detail.
Clothing with fine hatching — no colour fills.
Soft even lighting from sketch shading only.
White background, clean and empty.
Strong whitespace around the figure.
No vector aesthetic.
No flat illustration.
No cinematic lighting.
No painterly rendering.
No colour fills anywhere except single accent on orbit system.

COMPOSITION

Bust portrait, shoulder-to-head crop.
Subject slightly in 3/4 angle, looking thoughtfully up or away.
8 role-relevant icons arranged in dotted semicircular arc above and around the figure.
Each icon inside a thin circle outline.
Icons connected by dotted arc path with small solid circular dot markers at intervals.
Icons must be meaningful to the character's role and symbol language.
No typography.
No background elements.
No UI panels.
No hand gestures.
Hands not visible.

FACIAL DETAIL RULE

Realistic pencil sketch faces — eyes, nose, mouth, brow all rendered.
Natural expression aligned with character personality: calm, confident, thoughtful.
Fine crosshatching for skin texture.
Hair in grouped sketch masses.
No hyper-real pores or over-rendered skin.

ACCENT RULE

Single accent colour (Warm Ochre #C49A30) restricted ONLY to:
* icon circle borders
* dot markers along the orbit arc

Never use accent colour in:
* skin
* clothing
* hair
* background
* shading
* body of the figure
